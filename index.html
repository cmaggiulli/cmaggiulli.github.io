Welcome

Hi! You've somehow landed on my personal page. I originally developed this website for use in a Stack Overflow developer survey discussion (see my blog...) but have since repurposed it as my personal Github page. Most of the content has not been created yet, however I intend on using this site to list my current projects, host my resume, maintain a blog, and as a contact and interaction platform for past and potential clients. About Me


Resume
My name is Chris Maggiulli and I am a developer from the Albany New York and greater NYC area. I have a bachelors degree from the University at Albany with a concentration in computer science, 4 years of enterprise development and operations experience, and over 15 years working on academic, contract, or personal projects. I am currently employed as a Senior Java Developer for Latham International where I lead a team of 4 Java developers and work on Liferay, Hybris, and RabbitMQ platforms. My technical experience is in JVM languages, SCM, web services, cloud services, CI, build automation, relational data, agile tools, Linux RHEL, OOAD and patterns, MQ, unit and pen testing, , scripting, etc. As a team lead I (try to) estimate project timelines, research and develop new tech, mentor junior developers, onboard new Java developers, conduct technical interviews, and guide Latham's Java division. I am working on several personal projects and my goal for 2017 is to open source my past projects and start contributing code to the Liferay portal codebase.
