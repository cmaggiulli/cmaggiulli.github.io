About Me


Resume

My name is Chris Maggiulli and I am a developer from the Albany New York and greater NYC area. I have a bachelors degree from the University at Albany with a concentration in computer science. I am currently a Senior Software Engineer (Java) for Latham International Holdings, Inc.

I am a service matter expert in the open source Liferay platform with a fluency in JSR-286 portlet development (JSF, Struts2, Vaadlin, MVCPortlet, Spring, etc), Liferay Hooks (Adaptive Hooks, Model Listeners, Message Listeners, OSGi, Struts Actions Overrides, Index Post Processors, etc), Liferay Themes and Layouts (Bootstrap, SASS, AlloyUI and YUI, JQuery, LiferayUI and JSTL taglibs), Liferay Ext Plugins, Liferay DevOps (EhCache, Zookeeper, Maven, Ivy, Solr, Jenkins, Hibernate, OAuth, SVN, LDAP, etc), and Liferay Mobile SDK.

In addition to Liferay I also enjoy working with RabbitMQ, Redhat Linux, Tomcat and tcServer, MySql, AWS (Beanstalk, Lambda, EC2, DynamoDB, etc), PHP5, OpenStack, NodeJS, Groovy, BeanShell, Arduino, etc. I like working in well formed API's especially with DevTools Extension, Github, Atlassian, New York Open Data, Nasa Open Data, etc. I am interested in learning Scala and Hadoop.
